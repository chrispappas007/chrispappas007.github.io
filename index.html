Archaeological Cultures in Kentucky

Kentucky is often referred to as the 'Vacant Quarter', meaning there were few, if any, prehistoric cultures. This is a false assumption. This map explores the many prehistoric cultues of Native Peoples in Kentucky.

This map was generated for MAP 673 at the University of Kentucky.

  • See the map: Archaeological Cultures in Kentucky
  • To learn more: Kentucky Office of State Archaeology and Kentucky Heritage Council

Four Types of Powerplant

The choropleth map depicts four types of fuel used in US powerplants: wind, solar, hydroelectric, and petroleum. The cicle markers are scaled by the various fuel source's energy output.

This map was generated for MAP 672 at the University of Kentucky.

  • See the map: Four Types of Powerplant
  • To learn more: Data on the plant location, type, and output are drawn from U.S. Energy Information Adminstration.
